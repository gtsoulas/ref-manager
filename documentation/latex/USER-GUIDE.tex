\section{DOI Auto-Fetch (New in v4.0)}

Automatically populate output metadata from a DOI.

\subsection{Using DOI Auto-Fetch}

\begin{enumerate}
    \item Navigate to \textbf{Outputs $\rightarrow$ Add Output}
    \item In the \textbf{Intelligent Output Entry} section:
    \begin{itemize}
        \item Paste the DOI (e.g., \texttt{10.1038/nature12373})
        \item Click \textbf{Auto-Fill}
    \end{itemize}
    \item Review and adjust populated fields
    \item Select the Colleague and add ratings
    \item Click \textbf{Save}
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Supported DOI Formats}

All these formats work:
\begin{itemize}
    \item \texttt{10.1038/nature12373}
    \item \texttt{doi:10.1038/nature12373}
    \item \texttt{https://doi.org/10.1038/nature12373}
\end{itemize}

\section{O/S/R Quality Ratings (New in v4.0)}

Three-component quality assessment aligned with REF 2029.

\subsection{Rating Components}

\begin{table}[h]
\centering
\begin{tabular}{lp{8cm}}
\toprule
\textbf{Component} & \textbf{What to Assess} \\
\midrule
Originality & Innovation, new ideas, novel approaches \\
Significance & Importance, influence, reach, impact \\
Rigour & Quality of execution, methodology, evidence \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{table}

\subsection{Rating Scale}

\begin{table}[h]
\centering
\begin{tabular}{ccl}
\toprule
\textbf{Score} & \textbf{Star} & \textbf{Description} \\
\midrule
3.50 - 4.00 & 4$\star$ & World-leading \\
2.50 - 3.49 & 3$\star$ & Internationally excellent \\
1.50 - 2.49 & 2$\star$ & Internationally recognised \\
0.50 - 1.49 & 1$\star$ & Nationally recognised \\
0.00 - 0.49 & U & Unclassified \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{table}

\subsection{Rating Sources}

Each output can receive ratings from three sources:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Internal Panel} (blue card) -- Departmental reviewers
    \item \textbf{Critical Friend} (yellow card) -- External reviewers
    \item \textbf{Self-Assessment} (green card) -- Author's evaluation
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Entering Ratings}

\begin{enumerate}
    \item Edit an output
    \item Scroll to \textbf{Quality Ratings (O/S/R)} section
    \item Enter decimal values (0.00-4.00) for each component
    \item Save
\end{enumerate}

\section{Open Access Compliance (New in v4.0)}

Track REF 2029 Open Access requirements.

\subsection{OA Status Types}

\begin{table}[h]
\centering
\begin{tabular}{ll}
\toprule
\textbf{Status} & \textbf{Description} \\
\midrule
Gold & Published in full OA journal \\
Green & Available via repository (YORA) \\
Hybrid & OA article in subscription journal \\
Bronze & Free to read, no formal license \\
Closed & Paywalled \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{table}

\subsection{Compliance Dates}

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Acceptance Date}: When accepted by journal
    \item \textbf{Deposit Date}: When uploaded to repository
    \item \textbf{Embargo End Date}: When full-text becomes public
\end{itemize}

\subsection{3-Month Deposit Rule}

REF 2029 requires outputs to be deposited within 3 months (92 days) of acceptance.

\begin{itemize}
    \item \checkmark~\textbf{Compliant}: deposit\_date $-$ acceptance\_date $\leq$ 92 days
    \item $\times$~\textbf{Non-compliant}: deposit\_date $-$ acceptance\_date $>$ 92 days
\end{itemize}

\section{REF Narrative Statements (New in v4.0)}

\subsection{Double-Weighting Statement}

Required if output is marked as double-weighted.
\begin{itemize}
    \item Word Limit: 300 words
    \item Purpose: Justify why output merits double weighting
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Interdisciplinary Statement}

Optional for cross-panel outputs.
\begin{itemize}
    \item Word Limit: 500 words
    \item Purpose: Explain cross-panel methodology and value
\end{itemize}

\section{Bulk Import (New in v4.0)}

Enhanced bulk import with DOI lookup.

\subsection{Import Modes}

\begin{table}[h]
\centering

\toprule
\textbf{Mode} & \textbf{Description} \\
\midrule

\subsection{Running an Import}

\begin{enumerate}
    \item Navigate to \textbf{Outputs $\rightarrow$ Bulk Import}
    \item Select import mode
    \item Upload CSV file
    \item Enable options (skip duplicates, auto-link)
    \item Click \textbf{Start Import}
\end{enumerate}
